BAGO CITY COLLEGE STUDENTS EVENTS ATTENDANCE MONITORING SYSTEM WITH FACE RECOGNITION VIA ANDROID APPLICATION

The Event and Attendance Monitoring System with Face Recognition via Android Application is a cutting-edge solution for streamlining event management and attendance tracking, enhancing efficiency and accuracy through facial recognition technology in a user-friendly Android app. Implemented at Bago City College, this study addresses the necessity of an advanced attendance system for student events. Envision event organizers at Bago City College benefiting from streamlined processes, reducing administrative burdens, and ushering in a new era of enhanced event management.
